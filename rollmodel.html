CARTOON



Motu and Patlu are iconic characters with distinctive features and personalities that make them so memorable. **Motu**: - **Appearance**: Motu is typically portrayed with a large round belly, wearing a red tunic and a blue vest. He has a mustache and a bald head. - **Personality**: Motu is known for his love of samosas, and his primary goal is to find and eat as many as he can. He often gets into trouble due to his impulsive actions, but his heart is always in the right place. - **Strength**: Motu gains incredible strength when he eats samosas, which often helps him and his friends out of tricky situations. **Patlu**: - **Appearance**: Patlu is much slimmer compared to Motlu, and he is often seen wearing a yellow tunic and a brown vest. He has a mustache and wears spectacles. - **Personality**: Patlu is the brains of the duo, known for his intelligence and problem-solving skills. He often has to bail Motu out of trouble with his quick thinking. - **Strength**: While he doesn't have any special powers like Motu, his wisdom and resourcefulness make him an invaluable friend. Together, they navigate various adventures and challenges in the fictional town of Furfuri Nagar, bringing joy and laughter to their audience.
